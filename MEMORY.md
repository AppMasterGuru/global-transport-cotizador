## BASC Compliance — Key Facts (researched 2026-04-30)

- **BASC** = Business Alliance for Secure Commerce
- Supply chain security certification, originally backed by US Customs
- Globally administered by World BASC Organization (Miami); BASC Peru is local chapter
- Annual audits, recertification cycle
- **Vendor requirements** (per BASC Estándar Internacional 5.0.2 and 6.0.1):
  - Documented procedure for vendor selection and periodic review
  - Knowledge of partner's legal existence and ownership
  - Background checks (criminal, financial)
  - Operational monitoring
  - Anti-money-laundering and anti-terrorism-financing criteria
  - **Contractual commitment by suppliers to BASC security provisions**
- **ISO 9001 Clause 8.4** also applies — documented controls over external providers

**What Vania will likely send Barney for vendor registration:**
- Legal company documentation (RUC personal or RUC SAC)
- Identity documents
- Signed declaration of BASC compliance
- Data handling questionnaire (where systems hosted, who has access, credential storage)
- Possibly background-check disclosures

**What this means for the build:** BASC-grade credential handling from day one. Secrets manager, no plaintext, access logs, hosting region known, audit trail. Renato's BASC auditors may eventually look at how Global Transport's AI vendor handles their data.
